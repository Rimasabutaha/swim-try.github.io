Speedy Swimming Club.

SWIM, SWIM, SWIM!!

Our Swim club is not-for-profit organisations that is run by volunteers (usually parents of swimmers).Our Swim club organises and coordinate weekly meets (often in the evening), and provide a forum for our swimmers to race against other swimmers of comparable ability. The emphasis of these races is not on winning the race, but rather on swimmers achieving their personal best (PB).
